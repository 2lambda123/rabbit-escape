Rabbit Escape

A game of rescuing rabbits, inspired by Lemmings and Pingus.

Install

Control your rabbits by dropping tokens that give them special abilities.

See whether you can get them to the exit without killing them first!

Choose abilities like bridge-building, wall-climbing and digging, and drop tokens for your rabbits to pick up.
Help your rabbits find their way from the entrance to the exit.
If you rescue enough rabbits, you can go on the next level.
Create your own levels by editing simple text files in Notepad.
